In order to subscribe to the iCal do the following:
- Navigate to the Calendar App on your iPhone
- Open your list of calendars and scroll to the bottom
- Select "Add Calendar" and "Add Subscription Calendar"
- Paste the following link: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/misapprehending/gabba-calendar/refs/heads/main/gabba-events.ics
- You are now subscribed to the calendar
